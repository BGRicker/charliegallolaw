Practice Areas

Estate Planning

Gallo Law Office prepares both simple and complex estate plans for clients of all ages. Estate plans may include wills, trusts, health care proxies, powers of attorney, homesteads, nominations of guardians for minor children, and other legal documents.

Estate planning at Gallo Law Office interfaces frequently with Medicaid law and asset protection strategies that take into account the costs of nursing home care. Gallo Law Office helps clients to avoid loss of a home or depletion of savings to pay for long term care.

Gallo Law Office also prepares estate planning documents for children and adults with special needs or disabilities.

Elder Law

Gallo Law Office represents senior citizens and the families of senior citizens who are facing the costs of nursing home care.

Gallo Law Office also provides representation in guardianship and conservatorship proceedings involving senior citizens and younger adults with physical or mental disabilities, including Alzheimer's Disease and dementia.

Probate

Gallo Law Office handles probate and estate administration matters, including the probate of wills and estates following the death of a loved one. Gallo Law Office assists families in navigating the estate administration process.
