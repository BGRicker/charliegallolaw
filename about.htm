Practice Areas

Estate Planning

Gallo Law Office prepares both complex and simple estate plans that may include wills, trusts, health care proxies, powers of attorney, nominations of guardians for minor children, and homesteads.

Estate planning at Gallo Law Office interfaces frequently with Medicaid law and asset protection strategies, which take into account the costs of long term care. Gallo Law Office helps clients to avoid depletion of savings or loss of a home to pay for nursing home care.

Gallo Law Office also prepares estate planning documents for adults and children with special needs.

Elder Law

Gallo Law Office represents senior citizens and the families of senior citizens who are facing the costs of nursing home care.

Gallo Law Office also provides representation in guardianship and conservatorship proceedings involving senior citizens as well as younger adults with physical disabilities or mental illness, including Alzheimer's Disease and dementia.

Probate

Gallo Law Office handles probate and estate administration matters, including the probate of wills and intestate estates following the death of a loved one.

Non-Profits, Real Estate, and Tax Preparation

Gallo Law Office is available to represent new and existing non-profit corporations. Gallo Law Office also is available to represent buyers and sellers in residential real estate sales. Finally, Gallo Law Office offers income tax preparation for individuals, trusts, and estates.
